## 2. Brand Voice & Tone

### Voice Characteristics
- **Authoritative yet Approachable**: Command respect while remaining accessible
- **Results-Oriented**: Focus on outcomes, wins, and measurable success
- **Professional**: Maintain legal industry credibility and sophistication
- **Empowering**: Position clients as powerful advocates
- **Strategic**: Emphasize planning, purpose, and precision

### Tone Guidelines

**Do:**
- Use action-oriented language (Driving, Empowering, Building, Moving)
- Emphasize partnership and collaboration
- Focus on growth and expansion
- Highlight quality over quantity
- Use power words: Elite, Premium, Qualified, Strategic, Powerful

**Don't:**
- Sound generic or templated
- Over-promise or use hyperbole
- Use overly casual language
- Diminish the seriousness of legal work
- Focus solely on price/cost

## 8. Pricing Philosophy

### Positioning
**Premium but Accessible**: Our pricing reflects our quality while remaining accessible to growing firms.

### Messaging Around Price
**Do Say**:
- "Investment in your firm's growth"
- "Customized to your budget and goals"
- "ROI-focused pricing"
- "Flexible packages that scale with you"

**Don't Say**:
- "Cheap" or "budget-friendly"
- "One-size-fits-all"
- Focus exclusively on cost savings

### Price Presentation
- Always lead with value before price
- Frame as monthly investment, not expense
- Emphasize deliverables and outcomes
- Offer consultation to customize pricing
- Position higher tiers as growth accelerators

## 16. Brand Don'ts

### Never Do:
1. ❌ Promise specific case outcomes (legal ethics)
2. ❌ Use manipulative urgency tactics
3. ❌ Disparage other firms or competitors
4. ❌ Use generic stock photos of courtrooms
5. ❌ Overuse legal jargon that alienates
6. ❌ Focus solely on price/discounts
7. ❌ Make unsubstantiated claims
8. ❌ Ignore bar association marketing rules

### Always Do:
1. ✅ Lead with value and outcomes
2. ✅ Maintain professional credibility
3. ✅ Use clear, action-oriented language
4. ✅ Provide specific deliverables
5. ✅ Emphasize partnership and collaboration
6. ✅ Showcase real results (when permitted)
7. ✅ Offer consultation before hard sells
8. ✅ Respect attorney expertise
